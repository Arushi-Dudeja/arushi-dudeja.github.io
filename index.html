ABOUT ME

I’m a Master of Data Analytics graduate from Western University and a Data Analyst at Walmart Canada, passionate about building data and AI solutions that create real-world impact.

Previously, I applied predictive modeling in healthcare to optimize patient flow, and earlier in my career at EXL and Ernst & Young, I built analytics, anomaly detection, and automation solutions.

I enjoy working at the intersection of data, business, and emerging technologies, especially Generative AI, and I’m driven by curiosity, continuous learning, and turning complex problems into practical, data-driven solutions.
